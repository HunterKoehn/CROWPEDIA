About Us

We are the hotspot on everything crow.

We have general information about crows if you just want to know more about them, and guides if you want to become friends with the crows near you! Learn about what foods can optimize your chances at increasing your likeability with them and the best thing is that you can find tons to practice with practically anywhere you go! You can get in contact with us to learn about our vast community of like-minded individuals, or just peruse our messaging boards. We hope you enjoy your stay at CROWPEDIA!
